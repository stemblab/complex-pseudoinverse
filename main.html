Complex Pseudoinverse

The [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moore%E2%80%93Penrose_pseudoinverse, pseudoinverse] $A^+$ of a matrix $A$ can be computed from its SVD, $A=U \Sigma V^*$, as $$ A^+=V\Sigma^+U^* $$ Here $\Sigma^+$ is derived from $\Sigma$ by inverting its diagonal values whenever they excede a small tolerance, or otherwise setting them to zero. *Matrix $A$ must be complex in the code below*.
